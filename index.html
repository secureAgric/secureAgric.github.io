About us

We are a blockchain driven oil and gas commodity exchange platform, that gives oil and natural gas commodity traders a transparent platform to trade and exchange values, seamlessly and peer to peer with trust.

Transparency

We are making the Oil and Gas Commodity market more transparent, by leveraging Blockchain Technology. We see lack of transparency as one of the challange faced by traders in the energy commodity market, and we have challanged the status quo, by breaking the barriers towards achieving a more transparent energy for today and tomorrow.

Peer to Peer Transaction

We are changing the narrative in the Oil and Gas commodity market, by giving both parties an equal opportunity to exchange values without the inteferance of brokers in the transaction cycle. brokers and middle men are considered the major challange affecting the growth of the energy commodities market, thus affecting the economies of businesses and individuals that rely on the energy market for survival.

Trust

We are restoring trust and ensuring its sustainability in the oil and Gas commodity market, by eliminating scams and frauds and as well allowing commodity traders agree and transact based on simple and sustainable terms, through the use of technology. Our platform allow both parties talk to each other, set their terms based on some constant parameters and trade at at the spot.
